frislet

A flashcards studying tool with a 100% original name. Fun fact: it's a real word meaning something like ruffle. Quizlet? What's that? The premise of the tool is that instead of storing flashcard sets on paid servers, flashcard sets are portable archives with images and JSON. For example, a teacher could make a flashcard set and then distribute one file to all of their students for them to study.
I came up with it around the same time as pwamaker, but I've made even less progress on it. I have a problem with stalling...
